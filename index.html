The National Trust owns and manages over 775 miles of Britain’s beautiful coastline. We are a conservation charity, protecting green spaces and historic places, and opening them up for ever, for everyone. 2015 is the 50th anniversary of the national trust’s most successful campaign of all time - Neptune.

Over 50 years the people-powered Neptune Coastline Campaign has allowed the Trust to buy 574 miles of glorious coastline; securing these special places for everyone to enjoy. Hundreds of thousands of people have donated to our coastal appeals or supported our work on the coast, so that future generations can enjoy the beautiful, dramatic and diverse coast around England, Wales and Northern Ireland. We go to the coast to play, to relax and to connect with the natural world and the elements. Days at the seaside or walks along coastal cliffs are deeply engrained in our collective memory.

We are inviting you to make the most of the coast and take part in our ‘#CREATEYOURCOAST’ competition. The rules are simple: visit any coastal land in the UK and create a 20-60 second video with your friends and family; this could be anything - ranging from a dance video, or a scene from your favourite film, to something simple like a moment from your coastal adventure. Once you’re happy with your film, upload it to the National Trust’s ‘#CREATEYOURCOAST’ website, where you can share it with your friends, view other people’s videos, and vote on the videos you like the most. The top 5 winners of the competition will have their video showcased at our special event on August the 23rd, in addition to a year’s free membership with the National Trust.
